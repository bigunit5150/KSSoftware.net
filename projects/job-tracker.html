Open Source Project

One click to save any
job posting to Notion.

Job Tracker is a free Chrome extension that captures job postings from any job board and saves them as structured pages in your own Notion database — automatically extracting the role, company, location, and full job description.

Install from Chrome Web Store → View on GitHub →

Free  ·  No account required  ·  Your data stays in your own Notion workspace

How It Works

From job board to Notion
in under 3 seconds

01

Find a role

Browse job boards as you normally would — Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, LinkedIn, or any other site.

02

Click the icon

Click the Job Tracker icon in your Chrome toolbar. The extension captures the page and extracts the role details automatically.

03

It's in Notion

A structured page appears in your Notion database — role title, company, location, salary range, and the full job description, ready to review.

Features

Built for engineers who take
their job search seriously

Supports all major job boards

Built-in parsers for Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, and LinkedIn Jobs. Falls back gracefully to a generic extractor for any other site.

Never save the same role twice

When you open the extension on a posting you have already saved, the icon shows a yellow ring so you know before you click.

Smarter extraction with Claude

Add your own Anthropic API key to enable AI-powered extraction on job boards the built-in parser doesn't fully support.

Completely private by design

No accounts, no servers, no tracking. You supply your own Notion integration token and everything goes directly to your workspace.

Always know what's happening

The toolbar icon communicates status at a glance: idle, capturing, success, error, and duplicate — no need to open the popup.

Pipeline dashboard included

A companion Notion template gives you a live pipeline view, weekly goal tracking, and a status-filtered table of every role you are tracking.

Requirements

Designed for technical users

Job Tracker is a developer tool. It requires a free Notion account and a personal integration token — a one-time 5-minute setup. An Anthropic API key is optional and only needed for AI-powered extraction on lesser-known job boards.

  • Required Notion account (free)
  • Required Notion integration token (created at notion.so/my-integrations)
  • Optional Anthropic API key (for AI-powered extraction)
