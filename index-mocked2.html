Q&A

Why did you choose your profession?

I received my first computer at the age of 13 and have been hooked ever since. I am passionate about using technology to solve business problems, and the field of Software Engineering has enabled me to turn this passion into a fulfilling career.

Who are your main influences?

I find Kenneth Chenault, Charles Phillips, and Colin Powell to be influential role models in my career. Their exceptional accomplishments in their respective fields, dedication to leadership and innovation, and commitment to promoting diversity, economic opportunity, and responsible public service have significantly shaped my perspective.

What is your greatest accomplishment?

Lead architect of next generation network management system leveraged by the DoD to manage their global telephony network.

What tools do you use?

JIRA, Azure, AWS, Visual Studio Code, Viva Goals, AHA, Figma, Git Hub Copilot

What advice would you give to someone just starting out in your profession?

To be intentional with your engineering career, define your goals, continuously learn and improve, develop strong problem-solving skills, build a professional network, and pursue opportunities that align with your passions and long-term vision. This includes both technical and soft skills, and prioritizing a growth mindset.
