How It Works

Your AI coach,
every step of the way .

From finding a role to acing the interview — here’s what the journey looks like.

Phase 1 — Capture
01

Find a job you like

You’re browsing LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Lever, or any job board and spot a role that catches your eye.

02

Save it in one click

Click the CareerCopilot Chrome extension. The full posting — title, company, JD, location, salary — is saved to your private Notion database. No copy-pasting, nothing lost.

Phase 2 — AI Job Review
03

Your coach reviews the role

Your AI coach reads the full job description against your background and preferences. You get an honest fit assessment — where you’re strong, where the gaps are, and whether it’s worth pursuing.

04

A resume built for this role

If it’s a good match, your coach selects the right resume variant and optimizes it for this specific JD — adjusting language, reordering highlights, mirroring what the hiring team is looking for. The tailored resume is saved to your database.

05

You apply

Take the optimized resume and submit your application. You’re sending a resume that was built for this role, not a generic one-size-fits-all.

Phase 3 — Interview Prep & Coaching
06

You get the interview

Update the status in your Notion database. Your AI coach sees the change and interview prep begins.

07

Company research & cheat sheet

Your coach researches the company — recent news, culture, business model, why they’re hiring — and generates a master cheat sheet: your intro, key accomplishments mapped to the JD, STAR stories, gaps to acknowledge, questions to ask, and comp notes.

08

Interviewer-specific prep

Before each round, tell your coach who you’re meeting. They research the interviewer and tailor your talking points, questions, and stories for that specific person.

09

Mock interviews

Practice with your coach. Realistic questions based on the role and round type. Honest feedback on what landed, what to tighten, what to cut. Your cheat sheet updates with better framings.

10

Debrief after each round

After the real interview, debrief with your coach. Capture what went well, what surprised you, what to adjust. Your prep compounds — each round makes you sharper for the next.

11

Email review & draft responses

Your coach helps review follow-up emails and draft responses — thank-you notes, scheduling replies, negotiation responses — so every communication is polished and on-brand.

The Big Picture

A coach that
grows with you.

Every job you save, every review, every mock, every debrief makes your coach smarter about who you are. The system compounds over time — your preparation gets sharper, your confidence grows, and you show up better for every opportunity.
