The System

What each phase does.

Step 1

Save Jobs Instantly

Install the Chrome Extension. When you find a role on any job board, click the extension icon to save the full job description directly to your Notion job tracker — no copy-pasting.

Step 2

AI-Optimized Resumes

Claude reads each saved job description, evaluates fit against your background, selects the right resume variant, and rewrites it to match the role — without fabricating experience.

Step 3

Interview-Ready in Minutes

For every role you pursue, Claude generates a company research brief, a tailored cheat sheet, and runs mock interviews — all in a dedicated project that builds context over time.

What you'll need to get started:

  • A free Notion account (notion.so)
  • Google Chrome browser
  • A Claude account — Free tier works for setup; Pro recommended for daily use
  • Your existing resumes (Word, PDF, or text — any format)
